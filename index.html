Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining DEVCONF 2026.

Who can attend DEVCONF?

Anyone interested in software development, AI, cloud computing, startups, or modern technologies is welcome.

Will sessions be recorded?

Yes. All attendees will receive access to the recorded sessions after the conference.

Do I need to bring my laptop?

Bringing a laptop is recommended if you plan to participate in workshops and hands-on coding sessions.

Can I transfer my ticket?

Yes. Tickets can be transferred up to 7 days before the event begins.
